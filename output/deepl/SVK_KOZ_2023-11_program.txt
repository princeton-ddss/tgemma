The ambition of the trade union is to actively participate in ensuring that labor law protection guarantees employees a sufficient level of protection directly from the law and decent working conditions, in particular fair wages and salaries, safe work, equal treatment, professional growth, and enable them to balance their work and family lives.  The trade union is the largest organized interest group in Slovakia and is part of the most massive movement—the trade union movement—in Europe and the world.  X priorities of the trade union organization, which are based on value and program pillars and the anchoring of the trade union organization in European and Xnational trade union structures, as well as several position and expert materials and statements, include so-called "traditional" (bread-and-butter) issues such as wage and salary increases, bonuses, minimum wage, labor legislation, social security system including the pension system, reduction of working hours, reconciliation of family and working life, trade union organization and a healthy working environment, improving the working environment and eliminating psychosocial risks in the workplace, social dialogue and collective bargaining, trade union rights, strengthening employment, work-friendly tax policy, education policy and links to labor market needs, etc.
