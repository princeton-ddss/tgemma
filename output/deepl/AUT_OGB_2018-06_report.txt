Introduction: The work of many dedicated people The work and successes of the union are the result of the efforts of many dedicated people: in the union, in the trade unions, in companies, and in government agencies. There have been successes on both a small and large scale. This report describes the work of the union over the past period, focusing on its main areas of activity, and how each individual department and division of the union has contributed to our joint successes – from “Lower income tax!”, the successful membership recruitment campaign that brought the union an increase in membership for the first time in a long time, to hundreds of collective agreements, legislative reviews, and ongoing internal modernization.
