The trade union organization is the second largest trade union center in the Czech Republic. Together with X, it represents trade unions in the Economic and Social Agreement Council. This trade union center was established in July 1995 and its founders were X, X, and X. Over the following years, other trade unions joined this center, such as X, X, and X. Some independent trade unions and independent grassroots organizations also cooperate with the trade union organization. The trade unions affiliated with the trade union organization are politically independent, free, and have their own identity, with a total of approximately 200,000 members. Some unions have established regional offices.  The mission of the trade union organization is primarily to defend and promote the economic, labor, social, and other interests of the confederation's members, which are the individual trade unions affiliated with it, to assert the demands of the confederation's members in relation to the state, employers' associations, and other entities, to develop cooperation with partner foreign headquarters, cross-sector trade union organizations, and other international institutions, coordinating and defending the interests of confederation members at the national and regional levels.
