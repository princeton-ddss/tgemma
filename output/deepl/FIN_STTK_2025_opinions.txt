Skilled workers High-quality education that is accessible to all forms the basis for a civilized society.  Skills The trade union wants to strengthen everyone's right to learn throughout their lives and careers. Good skills and the opportunity for continuous learning during one's career strengthen equal and equitable opportunities to participate in working life.  Investing in skills strengthens employment, prevents exclusion, and improves equality and equity in society. Investing in skills and well-being at work creates the conditions for improving productivity.  The green transition and technological development are rapidly changing the skills requirements and occupational structures of working life. The importance of skills is therefore emphasized in ensuring a fair transition. Employees' actual opportunities to improve their own education and skills or to retrain for a new profession must be improved.  There is a shortage of skilled workers in almost all sectors.
