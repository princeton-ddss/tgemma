We are Argentina's leading trade union confederation. We bring together the largest number of unions from different sectors and branches of activity, acting on behalf of and defending workers' rights.  Since its founding on September 27, 1930, our organization has established itself as one of the most influential actors in the labor and political spheres. Our main objectives are: 1. Defense of Labor Rights: we seek to guarantee and defend workers' labor rights, including fair wages, safe working conditions, and social benefits.   2. Collective Bargaining: We facilitate collective bargaining with employers and the government to improve the working conditions and wages of our members. 3. Union Unity and Solidarity: We promote unity among affiliated unions and solidarity among workers to strengthen the labor movement.
