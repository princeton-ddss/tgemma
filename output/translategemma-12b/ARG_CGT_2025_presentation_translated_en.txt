We are the leading labor federation in Argentina. We represent the largest number of unions from various sectors and industries, acting in the representation and defense of workers' rights. Since our founding on September 27, 1930, our organization has become one of the most influential actors in the labor and political spheres. Our main objectives are:

1.  Defense of Workers' Rights: We strive to guarantee and defend the labor rights of workers, including fair wages, safe working conditions, and social benefits.
2.  Collective Bargaining: We facilitate collective bargaining with employers and the government to improve the working and wage conditions of our members.
3.  Union Unity and Solidarity: We promote unity among affiliated unions and solidarity among workers to strengthen the labor movement.
