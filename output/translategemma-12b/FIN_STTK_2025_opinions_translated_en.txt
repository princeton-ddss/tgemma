A skilled workforce and high-quality, accessible education are the foundation of societal progress. The trade union aims to strengthen everyone's right to learn throughout their lives and careers. Good skills and the opportunity for continuous learning during a career enhance equal opportunities for participation in the labor market. Investing in skills development promotes employment, prevents social exclusion, and improves equality and fairness in society. Investments in skills and employee well-being create the conditions for improving productivity. The green transition and technological advancements are rapidly changing the skills requirements and professional structures in the workplace. The importance of skills is particularly highlighted in ensuring a just transition. The actual opportunities for workers to improve their education and skills or to train for a new profession must be improved. There is a shortage of skilled workers in almost all sectors.
