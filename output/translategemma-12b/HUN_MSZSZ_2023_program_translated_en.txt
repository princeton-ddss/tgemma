Statement of Purpose: In response to the social, economic, and advocacy challenges of the 21st century, which differ from those previously experienced, and in order to increase the effectiveness of trade union work in protecting the interests of employees, the trade union, the trade union association, and the trade union federation are merging to form a new trade union. With this alliance, the three confederations are creating the largest trade union organization representing the majority of organized workers in the current Hungarian trade union movement. This new organization, and the complete integration of the three founding organizations, represents not only a responsibility but also a significant commitment. Our primary commitment is to the trade union membership, encompassing the entire workforce, including those in the competitive and public sectors, civil servants, public employees, members of armed and law enforcement organizations, those who have lost their jobs, and individuals with disabilities.
