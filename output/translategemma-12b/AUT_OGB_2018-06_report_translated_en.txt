Introduction: The Work of Many Dedicated Individuals

The work and achievements of the union are the result of the efforts of many dedicated individuals: within the union itself, in companies, and in various organizations. Successes are achieved both on a small and a large scale. This report describes the work of the past period, focusing on the union's main priorities, and explains how each department and section of the union has contributed to our shared successes – from "Lower Income Tax!", the successful membership recruitment campaign that has brought the union a membership increase for the first time in a long time, to hundreds of collective bargaining agreements, legislative reviews, and ongoing internal modernization efforts. Whether it was the development of the content for campaigns and key initiatives, their integration into union training programs, the communication and implementation, the information and advice provided to members, or the work of employee representatives, everyone has played a vital role.
