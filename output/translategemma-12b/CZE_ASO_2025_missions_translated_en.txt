The trade union organization is the second-largest trade union confederation in the Czech Republic. Together with X, it represents the unions in the Council for Economic and Social Agreement. This trade union confederation was founded in July 1995 by X, X, and X. Over the years, other trade unions have joined the confederation, including X, X, and X. The organization also collaborates with some independent trade unions and local branches. The trade unions affiliated with the organization are politically independent, free, and have their own identity, representing approximately 200,000 members. Some of the unions have established regional offices. The main objectives of the trade union organization are:

*   To defend and protect the economic, labor, and social interests of the members of the confederation, which are the individual trade unions within it.
*   To advocate for the demands of the confederation's members in relation to the state, employer associations, and other entities.
*   To foster cooperation with partner trade union organizations abroad, supranational trade union organizations, and other international institutions.
*   To coordinate and defend the interests of the confederation's members at both the national and regional levels.
