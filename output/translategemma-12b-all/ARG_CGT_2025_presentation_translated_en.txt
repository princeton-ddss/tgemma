We are the leading labor federation in Argentina. We represent the largest number of unions from various sectors and industries, acting in the representation and defense of workers' rights. Since our founding on September 27, 1930, our organization has established itself as one of the most influential actors in the labor and political spheres. Our main objectives are:

1.  Defense of Workers' Rights: We strive to guarantee and defend the labor rights of workers, including fair wages, safe working conditions, and social benefits.
2.  Collective Bargaining: We facilitate collective bargaining with employers and the government to improve the working and wage conditions of our members.
3.  Union Unity and Solidarity: We promote unity among affiliated unions and solidarity among workers to strengthen the labor movement.
4.  Political and Social Representation: We act as representatives of the interests of workers before the government and in the public sphere, influencing labor and social policies.
5.  Training and Education: We offer training and education programs to improve the skills and knowledge of workers, promoting their professional development.
6.  Social Protection: We work to ensure that working families have access to healthcare, education, housing, and other basic social services.
