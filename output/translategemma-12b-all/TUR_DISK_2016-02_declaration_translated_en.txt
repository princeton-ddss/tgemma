On February 12-14, 2016, the 15th conference of our union reaffirmed previous decisions and adopted the following resolution, based on union principles, to promote the rights of the working class and establish a free and democratic society:

1.  **A Democratic, Liberal, and Egalitarian Constitution:** We must demand a democratic, liberal, and egalitarian constitution and create an environment for democratic constitutional change. We will continue to fight against any authoritarian, oppressive, divisive, and restrictive form of government imposed by the government.

2.  **A Democratic and Peaceful Society:** We must overcome the growing polarization and conflict and defend democracy and peace in all areas, based on the brotherhood of the working class. Democracy and peace are the universal and indispensable goals of the working class, and our confederation is committed to fighting for these goals. We will fight for a peaceful resolution of the Kurdish issue based on equality, freedom, and fraternity.

3.  **Fair Distribution of Income:** We must ensure a just and balanced distribution of income by removing our country from the list of countries with the most unequal income distribution. Raising the minimum wage and expanding unionization and collective bargaining are our main goals.

4.  **Dignified and Secure Employment:** We will continue to fight in all areas to improve the working conditions of the working class and ensure dignified and secure employment. We will create a broad front of struggle to prevent informal employment, temporary work, and other forms of exploitation.

5.  **Migrant Workers:** We will work to ensure that Syrian migrant workers, who are part of the Turkish working class, are freed from inhumane working conditions and enjoy all rights, including access to public services, equal opportunities, and the right to organize, based on the principle of class solidarity. We will resolutely fight against racism, discrimination, and xenophobia.

6.  **Worker Health and Safety:** We will continue our efforts to ban precarious work, especially through subcontractors, to ensure that all workers have secure employment, remove obstacles to union freedom, and establish a new system with an autonomous and democratic structure involving unions, universities, and professional organizations.

7.  **Protection of Seniority Benefits:** We will protect the seniority benefits, which have been hard-won through long struggles and are an important pillar of job security. We will not allow any violation of our existing rights through any regulations on seniority benefits, and we will use all legitimate means to prevent this.
